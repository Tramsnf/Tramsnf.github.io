Artifact 01 | Featured

Career Launch Coach - an AI Career & Resume Assistant

A published ChatGPT custom GPT that helps job seekers turn real experience into strong resumes and confident interview answers.

Introduction

A no-code AI assistant that gives recent graduates and career changers accessible, judgment-free help with resumes and interview prep.

Description

A custom GPT with a coach persona, a curated resume best-practices knowledge base, and strict honesty constraints. It gathers real experience, rewrites it into results-focused bullets, tailors resumes to a posting, and coaches interview answers with the STAR method. Try the live assistant ->

Objective

To build an AI assistant end to end using Design Thinking, and deliver a genuinely useful tool that solves a real user problem.

Process

Ran the full Design Thinking cycle (Empathize -> Define -> Ideate -> Plan -> Prototype -> Test). Wrote the system instructions, uploaded a best-practices knowledge file, then tested against defined rules - catching a case where an early version invented experience and fixing it with a no-fabrication rule before publishing.

Tools & Technologies

ChatGPT custom GPT builder, Markdown knowledge base, Design Thinking framework.

Value Proposition

Shows the ability to design, constrain, test, and ship a responsible AI product - not just train a model.

Unique Value

Ethics are built into the instructions: it never fabricates experience, never advises on protected characteristics, and reminds users to protect confidential data.

Relevance

Directly transferable to building enterprise AI assistants and deploying responsible, trustworthy AI at work.

References

Live GPT link (above); OpenAI GPT Builder; AI Lab (AIML-500) coursework.

Artifact 02

The Evolution of Artificial Intelligence (1950-2026)

A visual, chronological timeline of AI's milestones, its two "AI winters," and the modern deep-learning era.

Introduction

A research-driven timeline tracing AI from the Turing Test to today's reasoning models and AI agents.

Description

A color-coded vertical timeline covering the Dartmouth Conference, the perceptron, ELIZA, both AI winters, Deep Blue, AlexNet, the Transformer, ChatGPT, and the 2025-2026 shift to reasoning models and agents. View the timeline ->

Objective

To research, synthesize, and clearly communicate the historical development of AI and machine learning.

Process

Researched milestones across credible sources, organized them chronologically, designed the timeline in Lucidchart, and iterated on clarity and accuracy.

Tools & Technologies

Lucidchart; secondary research.

Value Proposition

Shows the ability to research, synthesize, and communicate complex technical history in a clear, visual form.

Unique Value

Frames AI history as a boom-and-bust cycle, highlighting the "AI winters" that many overviews skip.

Relevance

Provides the historical grounding needed to reason about where AI stands today and where it is heading.

References

IEEE Computer Society; Our World in Data; "The Entire History of AI" (YouTube).
